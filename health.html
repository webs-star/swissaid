Health & Wellness

Promoting Healthy Communities

Our health programs improve access to essential medical services, health education, and critical interventions for vulnerable populations. We focus on disease prevention, maternal and child health, mental wellness, and community outreach.

Medical Outreach Programs

We organize mobile clinics, vaccination drives, and health awareness campaigns to reach underserved communities. Our efforts provide lifesaving care and health education to those in need.

Field Visits by County:

  • Nairobi: Kenyatta National Hospital, Mbagathi Hospital, Kariobangi Health Centre, Nairobi Women’s Hospital.
  • Murang’a: Murang’a Level 5 Hospital, Makuyu Health Centre, Kangari Health Centre.
  • Kiambu: Kiambu County Referral Hospital, Limuru Health Centre, Thika Level 5 Hospital.
  • Nakuru: Nakuru Level 5 Hospital, Kapkures Health Centre, Naivasha Sub-County Hospital.
  • Machakos: Machakos Level 5 Hospital, Matuu Sub-County Hospital, Kangundo Health Centre.
  • Mombasa: Mombasa County Referral Hospital, Port Reitz District Hospital, Likoni Sub-County Hospital.

Maternal and Child Health

We provide specialized programs for maternal care, early childhood development, nutrition, and immunization. Through partnerships with local hospitals and health centers, we ensure healthier mothers and children in Nairobi, Murang’a, Kiambu, Nakuru, Machakos, and Mombasa.

Mental Health & Wellness

Our initiatives promote mental wellness through awareness, counseling, community support, and training programs. We collaborate with health facilities across all counties to reduce stigma and support holistic health in society.

Engaging Youth in Health

We educate young people on healthy lifestyles, disease prevention, and community health leadership. Youth-led health campaigns empower the next generation to champion wellness through school visits, mentorship, and hands-on volunteering in hospitals and community clinics.
