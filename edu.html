Hybrid Education Centre for Youth

Hybrid Education Overview

We provide flexible digital and in-school learning support to children and youth across Nairobi, Murang’a, Kiambu, Nakuru, Machakos, and Mombasa. Our hybrid model combines technology access, community-based tutoring, skills development, and school support, ensuring that every learner can access quality education.

Through online modules, mentorship, practical fieldwork, and structured study sessions, students gain knowledge, confidence, and digital literacy to succeed in the 21st century.

Why Hybrid Learning?

Kenya faces large education gaps: overcrowded classrooms, limited digital access in rural and peri-urban areas, high cost of online learning, and lack of after-school support. Hybrid education bridges these gaps by combining:

  • Digital lessons accessible online and offline
  • Community learning hubs and study centers
  • School-based tutoring and mentorship
  • Home-based support through worksheets and mobile tutors
  • Skill development in ICT, entrepreneurship, and STEM

Counties We Serve

Our programs are active in:

  • Nairobi: Urban and informal settlement learners, digital clubs, safe weekend hubs, ICT skills and youth empowerment.
  • Murang’a: Rural learners, community hybrid hubs, girls' mentorship, school tree planting, environmental education.
  • Kiambu: High youth population, digital literacy classes, coding, after-school tutoring centers.
  • Nakuru: Mixture of rural and urban learners, STEM mentorship, hybrid labs, teacher capacity-building.
  • Machakos: Distance challenges addressed by hybrid model, weekend digital lessons, after-school hubs.
  • Mombasa: Coastal youth, blended learning, vocational skills, girls empowerment, community study centers.

Program Activities

  • Digital Learning Access: Tablets, online/offline lessons, low-data solutions.
  • Community Hybrid Learning Hubs: Safe spaces with internet, mentors, quiet study zones, girls' STEM clubs.
  • School Visits & Support: Teacher training, mentorship, digital labs, study materials.
  • Youth Skills Development: ICT, coding, digital marketing, entrepreneurship, career guidance.
  • Home-Based Support: Mobile tutors, worksheets, radio/audio lessons, WhatsApp homework help.

Program Impact

Across the six counties, our hybrid education program has:

  • Reached hundreds of learners with digital and physical support
  • Established multiple community learning hubs
  • Trained teachers and youth mentors
  • Provided devices and learning materials to students
  • Empowered girls in STEM and leadership programs
  • Improved exam readiness, digital literacy, and vocational skills
  • Integrated environmental education through school tree planting initiatives
