Rapid Crisis Response

We deliver timely and compassionate assistance to individuals and families affected by disasters, conflicts, and emergencies. Our response programs focus on immediate lifesaving support including emergency food distribution, temporary shelter, essential relief items, and urgent medical referrals. We also provide inclusive protection services for persons with disabilities and psychosocial support for youth affected by trauma, substance abuse, and displacement, helping them restore dignity and stability.

  • ✓ Emergency food assistance
  • ✓ Temporary shelter and emergency relief
  • ✓ Essential non-food relief items (blankets, kits, supplies)
  • ✓ Hygiene and dignity kits distribution
  • ✓ Medical referrals and emergency care support
  • ✓ Protection and psychosocial support for vulnerable groups

Food & Nutrition Assistance

We provide emergency food relief and long-term nutrition support to vulnerable households facing hunger and food insecurity. Our programs include food distribution, school feeding support, maternal and child nutrition programs, and community education on healthy diets. We also strengthen household resilience through livelihood support and sustainable food practices.

  • ✓ Emergency food distribution
  • ✓ School feeding support programs
  • ✓ Maternal and child nutrition support
  • ✓ Community nutrition awareness campaigns
  • ✓ Household livelihood strengthening support
  • ✓ Sustainable food security initiatives

Safe Shelter & Protection

We provide safe and dignified shelter solutions for individuals and families displaced by disasters, poverty, or conflict. Our programs include emergency accommodation, transitional housing, and community-based protection spaces. We also ensure safe environments for vulnerable groups, including women, children, persons with disabilities, and youth in recovery programs.

  • ✓ Emergency accommodation support
  • ✓ Transitional and recovery housing
  • ✓ Safe community protection spaces
  • ✓ Protection services for women and children
  • ✓ Disability-inclusive shelter support
  • ✓ Safe recovery environments for youth

Livelihood & Economic Recovery

We support vulnerable individuals and families to rebuild their livelihoods after crisis situations. Our programs focus on skills training, small business support, vocational empowerment, and entrepreneurship development. We help communities regain financial independence through practical income-generating activities and access to economic opportunities.

  • ✓ Skills development and vocational training
  • ✓ Youth and women entrepreneurship support
  • ✓ Small business startup assistance
  • ✓ Financial literacy training
  • ✓ Income-generating project support
  • ✓ Community economic empowerment programs

Drug Abuse Prevention & Rehabilitation

We run prevention campaigns, counseling services, and rehabilitation support programs for youth affected by substance abuse. Our approach includes awareness creation, behavioral change programs, counseling, peer support groups, and structured recovery pathways. We also support reintegration into society through skills development, mentorship, and livelihood empowerment.

  • ✓ Awareness campaigns in schools and communities
  • ✓ Counseling and psychosocial support
  • ✓ Peer support and recovery groups
  • ✓ Rehabilitation and recovery programs
  • ✓ Skills training and mentorship
  • ✓ Reintegration and livelihood support

Disability Support & Inclusion

We promote inclusion and equal opportunity for persons with disabilities through assistive support, inclusive education, skills development, and empowerment programs. Our focus is to ensure full participation in society through training, mentorship, and access to community opportunities.

  • ✓ Assistive devices and accessibility support
  • ✓ Inclusive education programs
  • ✓ Skills training and capacity building
  • ✓ Youth mentorship and leadership development
  • ✓ Digital skills and empowerment programs
  • ✓ Community participation and inclusion initiatives
