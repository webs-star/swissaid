Humanitarian Aid

Rapid Crisis Response

We deliver timely, compassionate assistance to individuals and families affected by disasters, conflicts, and hardship. Our programs provide lifesaving support including food, clean water, shelter, hygiene supplies, and inclusive support for people with disabilities and drug abuse recovery programs.

Food & Nutrition Assistance

We distribute food supplies and nutrition support to vulnerable communities. Programs are county-specific for better reach:

  • Nairobi: Kibera, Mathare
  • Muranga: Kandara, Maragua
  • Kiambu: Thika, Ruiru
  • Nakuru: Njoro, Naivasha
  • Machakos: Machakos town, Mwala
  • Mombasa: Likoni, Kisauni

Safe Shelter & Housing

Temporary shelters, tents, and reinforced housing are provided to protect families. Programs also cater to disability-inclusive shelters and safe spaces for youth recovering from drug abuse.

  • Nairobi: Informal settlements
  • Muranga & Kiambu: Community shelters
  • Nakuru: Displaced families support
  • Machakos: Rural communities
  • Mombasa: Coastal informal settlements

Clean Water & Hygiene

We ensure access to safe water and hygiene facilities, with special attention to accessibility for persons with disabilities.

  • Nairobi: Kibera, Mathare
  • Muranga: Maragua, Kandara
  • Kiambu: Ruiru, Thika
  • Nakuru: Naivasha, Njoro
  • Machakos: Machakos town, Mwala
  • Mombasa: Likoni, Kisauni

Drug Abuse Prevention & Rehabilitation

We provide awareness campaigns, counseling, and rehabilitation for youth. Programs are tailored per county:

  • Nairobi: Mathare Youth Rehabilitation Center
  • Muranga: Kandara & Maragua youth rehab
  • Kiambu: Thika youth rehab
  • Nakuru: Nakuru town youth centers
  • Machakos: Machakos town youth rehab
  • Mombasa: Likoni youth programs

Disability Support & Youth Engagement

We provide assistive devices, training, inclusive education, and mentorship for youth-led community projects. Programs aim to build resilience and promote leadership among young people while supporting persons with disabilities.

  • Nairobi: Nairobi Rehabilitation Center for Disabilities
  • Muranga & Kiambu: Community support programs
  • Nakuru: Nakuru County disability initiatives
  • Machakos: Inclusive education programs
  • Mombasa: Likoni & Kisauni support centers
