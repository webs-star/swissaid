Overview of Youth & Women Empowerment

Our programs focus on actionable youth and women empowerment initiatives, including mentorship, leadership development, life skills, civic engagement, education, entrepreneurship, and digital inclusion. We aim to equip young people and women in Moyale, Nairobi, Muranga, Kiambu, Nakuru, Machakos, and Mombasa with the knowledge, confidence, and opportunities needed to thrive socially, economically, and digitally.

Mentorship & Leadership Development

Our mentorship and leadership programs empower youth and women through guidance, capacity building, and leadership training in Moyale, Nairobi (Kibera community hubs), Muranga (Kandara community projects), and Kiambu (Thika & Ruiru initiatives). These programs promote civic responsibility, gender equality, confidence building, drug abuse prevention, and community-driven advocacy.

Entrepreneurship & Economic Opportunities

Youth and women business incubation, vocational training, microfinance support, and entrepreneurship programs are implemented in Kiambu, Mombasa, and Nairobi. These initiatives provide mentorship, start-up support, financial literacy, access to markets, and digital skills, empowering participants to create sustainable businesses, secure employment, and improve their livelihoods.

Sports, Arts & Civic Engagement

Youth and women actively participate in sports, arts, culture, volunteerism, and civic engagement programs. Nairobi and Nakuru host competitive leagues and creative arts events; Machakos and Muranga focus on leadership and community service projects; Mombasa promotes entrepreneurship and social innovation; while Kiambu encourages creativity, innovation, and active citizenship.
